EA-SA-001
Service Agreement
Agreed and signed by the client (or family representative) before the service begins. Sets out expectations for both parties.
Note: This form sets out the terms of the service. Please read carefully before signing. Everyday Advocates is a non-regulated social support service and does not provide personal care, medical care, or regulated financial advice.
Parties
Services Agreed
Key Terms

1. Scope of service. Everyday Advocates provides social support, companionship, and life administration assistance. We do not provide regulated personal care, medical advice, or financial advice.

2. Confidentiality. All information shared by the client is held confidentially and will not be shared with third parties without consent, except where there is a concern for safety.

3. Cancellations. Please give at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule a visit. Late cancellations may incur a charge.

4. Boundaries. Advocates will not handle client finances directly (e.g. hold cash, access bank accounts) or perform tasks outside the agreed scope without discussion.

5. Safeguarding. If an advocate has any concern for a client's safety or welfare, they are obligated to escalate appropriately, including to emergency services if necessary.

6. Termination. Either party may end this agreement with 2 weeks' written notice.
