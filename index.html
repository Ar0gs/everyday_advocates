Everyday Advocates was founded with one belief: that older people deserve consistent, dignified, and genuinely helpful support — and that their families deserve peace of mind.

Our story

Why I started Everyday Advocates.

I've spent years working in business analysis and wellbeing — and I kept noticing the same gap. Older people often needed support that wasn't clinical — not a carer, not a nurse, just someone reliable and warm who could help with the bits of life that become harder over time.

The post that sits unopened. The phone that's too confusing. The week that goes by without a single proper conversation.

I also knew, from my background in wellbeing, how much those things matter. Loneliness, loss of independence, the identity shift that comes with getting older — these are real, and they deserve a real response.

So I started Everyday Advocates — a service built around what older people and their families actually need, delivered by people who genuinely care about getting it right.

Everyday Support

Our founding service. Practical, personal, and built for the everyday. Everyday Support is available now across Coventry and surrounding areas.

01

Companionship & Wellbeing Visits

Loneliness is one of the biggest challenges facing older people today — and one of the least talked about. Regular, meaningful social connection makes a real difference to mood, confidence, and overall wellbeing.

Our companionship visits are relaxed and personal. We might share a cup of tea and a conversation, take a gentle walk, visit a local café, tend to the garden, or simply sit together and watch the world go by.

What's included

  • Weekly or fortnightly visits at a time that suits
  • Outings — walks, cafés, local events
  • Meaningful conversation and a genuine listening ear
  • Light emotional support and wellbeing check-ins
  • Consistent advocate — you'll always see a familiar face
From £20/hour Monthly packages available
02

Life Admin & Support

For many older people — and their families — it's the paperwork that causes the most stress. A letter from the council. A renewal form. A GP referral that needs chasing. Bills that are confusing. We help clients work through all of it — calmly, systematically, and at their own pace.

Please note: We help clients understand and manage their admin — we do not provide legal or financial advice, and we never handle client finances directly.

What's included

  • Help understanding official letters and documents
  • Support completing forms (benefits, housing, NHS, etc.)
  • Booking and chasing GP, hospital, and other appointments
  • Organising paperwork and correspondence
  • Liaising with relevant organisations on the client's behalf (with consent)
From £20/hour Monthly packages available
03

Tech Support for Seniors

Technology can be a lifeline — video calls with grandchildren, NHS app appointments, WhatsApp groups with friends. But only if you know how to use it. We help clients get confident with the devices they already have, at a pace that feels comfortable and without any judgement.

What's included

  • Setting up and getting started with smartphones and tablets
  • Learning to use WhatsApp, FaceTime, and video calling
  • Navigating the NHS App and online services
  • Safe internet use and scam awareness
  • Ongoing help as new questions come up
From £25/session 90 minutes · Block bookings available

Getting started

Getting started
is simple.

From your first enquiry to your first visit, we keep things clear, unhurried, and completely tailored to you.

Our six-step process

01
✉️

Get in touch

Fill in our short enquiry form, send us an email, or give us a call. Tell us a little about what you're looking for — or just that you'd like to find out more. There's no commitment at this stage.

02
💬

Free consultation

We'll arrange a free consultation — by phone, video call, or in person, whichever you prefer. This is a relaxed conversation to understand the situation, answer any questions, and work out whether we're the right fit.

03
📋

We create your personal plan

If you'd like to go ahead, we'll put together a simple service plan tailored to your needs — agreeing on the services, schedule, and any specific preferences. We'll also complete a short client profile so your advocate knows exactly how to support you well from day one.

04
🤝

Meet your advocate

Before visits begin, we'll introduce you to the advocate who'll be working with you. Getting that first connection right matters — so we take care to match clients and advocates thoughtfully.

05
🌟

Your service begins

Visits start at the agreed time. After each session, your advocate completes a brief summary note — so there's always a record, and any follow-up actions are tracked. Families can be kept updated too, with the client's consent.

06
🔄

Regular reviews

We check in regularly to make sure the service is still working well. As needs change, so can the plan.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Who we are

Everyday Advocates is a social support service based in Coventry, providing companionship, life admin help, and practical support for older people. Our contact email is hello@everydayadvocates.co.uk.

2. What information we collect

When you submit an enquiry via our Contact form, we collect your name, email address, phone number (if provided), and the details of your enquiry. We also collect information about the type of enquiry and service you're interested in, and whether you've given consent for us to contact you.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (such as health information) through this website.

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry and, where relevant, to follow up about our services. We do not use your data for marketing without your explicit consent, and we do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your data on the basis of your consent (given when you submit the enquiry form and tick the consent checkbox) and our legitimate interest in responding to enquiries about our services.

5. How long we keep your data

We retain enquiry data for up to 12 months. If you become a client, your data will be held in accordance with our client data retention policy, which we will share with you at the start of your service.

6. Where your data is stored

Enquiry data submitted through this website is stored securely in Supabase, a cloud database service. Data is stored within the European Economic Area (EEA).

7. Cookies

This website uses a small number of functional cookies — specifically to remember your light/dark theme preference. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. If you accept analytics cookies, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. You can change your cookie preferences at any time using the banner at the bottom of this page.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You also have the right to object to processing or to withdraw your consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, please email us at hello@everydayadvocates.co.uk.

9. Contact & complaints

If you have any concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us at hello@everydayadvocates.co.uk. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
